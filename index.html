First episode that sets up the world and hooks viewers.

🎞️ Short Film

Focused adaptation of a key scene or chapter.

Target: 10–30 pages · 10–30 min
Compress — Every subplot and character arc from your source is preserved, but scenes are tightened, dialogue is trimmed, and descriptions are cut to fit the target page count. Best when your story is already well-structured and you want nothing left out.
Select Best — The AI identifies the most cinematic, dramatic, and emotionally powerful scenes from your source and builds the screenplay around those. Weaker subplots may be dropped entirely. Best for sprawling books where ruthless editing is needed.
Faithful — Stays as close to the source material as possible — same scene order, same dialogue where practical, same pacing. Ideal when you have an existing adaptation contract or need to stay true to the book for rights reasons.
